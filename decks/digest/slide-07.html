The Last Mile of Education

07

Access is solved.

MIT OpenCourseWare. YouTube. arXiv. The best lectures in history are free.

Understanding is not.

A professor can't ask every student "explain it back to me." There's 300 of them in the lecture hall.

AI can.

Every student. Every concept. Infinite patience.

Who Benefits

Students

Paste lecture PDF → quiz yourself → tutor catches gaps → actually pass the exam

Developers

Paste technical blog → mindmap architecture → quiz edge cases → explain to teammates

Lifelong Learners

Paste YouTube talk → podcast for commute → flashcards for spaced review

Educators

Generate quiz + cards from any material instantly. AI tutor handles 1-on-1.
